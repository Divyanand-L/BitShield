## ANALYSIS REQUIREMENTS

For optimal fraud detection analysis, ensure:

1. **Text Length Requirements:**
   - Executive Summary: Minimum 200 words
   - Technical Proposal: Minimum 500 words
   - Company Qualifications: Minimum 300 words
   - Terms and Conditions: Minimum 200 words

2. **Writing Style Consistency:**
   - All narrative sections must be written in complete sentences
   - Use professional business language
   - Maintain consistent terminology throughout
   - Avoid copying large blocks of text from external sources

3. **Pricing Transparency:**
   - Provide itemized breakdown of all costs
   - Show clear calculation methodology
   - Include all assumptions and exclusions
   - Specify currency and validity period

4. **Contact Information Accuracy:**
   - Provide verifiable contact details
   - Use official company email domains
   - Include physical business address (not P.O. Box only)
   - Ensure phone numbers are functional

5. **Unique Content:**
   - All narrative sections must be original
   - Generic boilerplate should be minimal (<10% of content)
   - Company-specific details must be prominent
   - Project-specific customization required

## SUBMISSION GUIDELINES

1. **File Format:** Submit as PDF with text layer (not scanned images)
2. **File Naming:** `[TenderID]_[CompanyName]_Proposal.pdf`
3. **Page Limit:** Maximum 50 pages (excluding attachments)
4. **Font Requirements:** Minimum 10pt font, standard fonts (Arial, Times, Calibri)
5. **Language:** English (or specified tender language)
6. **Submission Deadline:** As specified in tender document
7. **Digital Signature:** Required on page 1 (Bidder Information section)

## FRAUD DETECTION NOTE

**Important:** This document structure enables automated fraud detection analysis including:
- **Price Analysis:** Identifies statistically unusual pricing patterns
- **Similarity Detection:** Detects copied content between bidders
- **Stylometry Analysis:** Identifies common authorship across proposals
- **Relationship Mapping:** Detects coordination between bidders

All submissions will be analyzed for compliance, originality, and consistency. Proposals showing signs of collusion, bid-rigging, or fraudulent content will be flagged for investigation.
